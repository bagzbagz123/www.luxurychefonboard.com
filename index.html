The Pinnacle of Yacht Dining

Chef Renata crafts world-class gourmet experiences to elevate every yacht charter.

learn more

In the luxury yacht market, Chef Renata sets the standard for professional private dining in Miami.

Nothing compares to having a private executive gourmet chef prepare a feast fit for royalty aboard a luxury yacht on the South Florida waterways. And for term charters, Chef Renata ensures unforgettable culinary indulgence throughout your entire voyage to the neighboring islands.
‍
Private Yacht Dining Excellence Chef Renata brings mastery across diverse cuisines, combined with expertise in galley safety, sanitation, and provisioning. Every menu is tailored to her clients’ preferences, with the flexibility to adapt dishes to changing locations, weather, or occasions at sea.

Menu Designed Specifically For Your Tastes and Requirements.

It all starts with a review of your culinary fancy of desired tastes and omissions (in case of any food allergies or personal preferences).
‍
Any dietary requirements can be catered to on an individual basis. Whether you’re vegan, vegetarian, carnivore, allergic to nuts etc., everything is made possible for your individual experience. Each meal is extensively planned and designed around your instructions, taste and Chefs imagination and skills.
